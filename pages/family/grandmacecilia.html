Grandmother Cecilia Theresa Young Hurley

My mom and I were visiting my grandmother. My grandmother wanted to visit my mother. I visited my grandmother’s sister. A spirit visited my grandmother. My grandfather visited my grandmother. My grandmother saw her sister. My grandmother’s sister visited my grandmother. My grandmother sees her sister. My mother is now my grandmother’s sister. My grandmother misses her daughter.














I am the spirit who visited my grandmother.
